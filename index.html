      Hints & Tips

      • Ace is always the highest card.
      • Click Draw to play each round.
      • Check the card value reference below for rankings.

      How to Play

      • Click New Deck to start a new game.
      • Click Draw to draw two cards: one for Computer, one for You.
      • The higher card wins the round and earns a point.
      • If both cards are equal, it's a "War!" and no points are awarded.
      • When all cards are drawn, the player with the most points wins the game.

      Winner/Loser: At every draw, the winner is the player with the higher card. At the end, the player with the highest score is the overall winner.
